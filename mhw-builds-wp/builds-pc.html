Charge Blades worth crafting

Saber's Maw II For story progression. Later, you can make it into Glavenus CB. Chrome Fortress III Great all-purpose CB until it is replaced by Gold Rathian CB. Can use Elementless. Dear Rosetta Very similar to Chrome Fortress III, trading 10 raw and elementless for better slots, 10% affinity, and high poison. Later, it upgrades into Luna Eostra. Dante's Devil Sword+ EVENT ONLY! Stronger than Chrome Fortress III and Dear Rosetta but weaker than Luna Eostra. Can be used for progression as it can be obtained much earlier than Luna Eostra. Royal Star Shield EVENT ONLY! Stronger than Chrome Fortress III and Dear Rosetta but weaker than Luna Eostra. Can be used for progression as it can be obtained much earlier than Luna Eostra. The Melting Grasp For high phial damage builds. Can get purple sharpness but CB cannot hold it even with Master's Touch because of sharpness loss from blocking. Can use Elementless. Luna Eostra This is currently the best impact charge blade. High raw, positive affinity, great sharpness, great slots. Not elementless but has very high poison. Must be MR70+ to obtain. Despot's Thundergale Best Thunder elemental CB (pre-Safi) Deep Schnegel II Best Ice elemental CB (even after Safi) Glavenus Bardred Highest Fire Element CB (pre-Safi) Hellsire Blade Notes: Lower Fire Element but higher affinity and raw than Glavenus Deep Terroir II Best Water elemental CB.. but pretty bad still (pre-Safi) Deathaxe Vaal Aspida Best Dragon elemental CB (pre-Safi)

Safi'Jiiva Impact Builds

Safi FAQ

Which Safi CBs should I get?

For impact CBs, I recommend blast as the best general purpose CB. Sleep is also good for multiplayer or farming shinies in Guiding Lands. Poison feels worse than blast and paralysis takes a very long time to proc without investment into status. For elemental CBs, all but ice are best in slot for their element. Ice is beaten by Beotodus CB.

#Master's Touch (no Teo essence)

Build variants: Guard 5 Artillery 0 Artillery 0, Focus 0

#Master's Touch (Teo essence)

Almost the same damage as without Teo essence, but the CB can be reused for Safi/Teo or Zorah/Teo sets.

Build variants: Guard 5 Artillery 0 Artillery 0, Focus 0

#Master's Touch (Gamma helm)

Requires Kaiser Gamma helm (Arch Tempered Teostra) which sacrifices some defense, but allows the strongest impact CB builds.

Build variants: Guard 5 Artillery 0 Artillery 0, Focus 0

#Safi + Teo Hybrid

Slightly weaker than Gamma builds and has Safi health drain, but higher defense and status. Also good if you prefer Resentment to Peak Performance.

Build variants: Artillery 0 Artillery 0, Focus 0

#Zorah + Teo Hybrid

This build is extremely inflexible and cannot use more than Guard 1. But it's here for phial damage.

#Anti-Safi

I use this vs Safi'jiiva. Mind's Eye can be a good substitution as the bouncing on legs is extremely annoying.
